SF1000

The Ferrari SF1000, introduced for the 2020 Formula 1 season, is a car that commemorates a historic milestone for Scuderia Ferrari: their 1,000th Grand Prix. This car embodies Ferrari’s rich heritage and commitment to innovation, while striving to maintain their competitive edge in the modern era of Formula 1.

Driven by Charles Leclerc and Sebastian Vettel, the SF1000 faced a challenging season due to regulatory changes and the highly competitive nature of the grid. Despite these hurdles, the car demonstrated flashes of potential, with Leclerc securing notable podium finishes. The team’s efforts with the SF1000 highlighted their resilience and dedication to continuous improvement, even in the face of adversity.

Today, the Ferrari SF1000 is recognized as a symbol of Ferrari's rich heritage and innovative spirit. It stands as a testament to the team's dedication to pushing the boundaries of technology and performance, even during challenging times. The SF1000 remains an integral part of Ferrari’s journey, reflecting their ongoing pursuit of glory in Formula 1.
